Puff Dat Magic Praline

Pralines infused with cannabis

Sugar, butter, and cream slow cooked to a stiff caramel with pecans... and weed!

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cups white sugar
  • 1 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup half & half
  • 8 tbsp salted cannabutter
  • 1/2 tbsp vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Combine cannabutter,sugar, and half and half into a large saucepan, then the heat up to meadium.
  2. Bring the candy mixture to 240 F, and let the candy for 5 minutes without stirring.
  3. Add vanilla and stir.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Fold in pecans.
  6. Spoon candy onto parchment paper.
  7. Let candy cool completely.
  8. Sugar High Time!
