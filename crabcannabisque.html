Crab Cannabisque

A cannabis infused crab bisque

Get high on this rich and creamy entree!

New Orleans Holy Trinity (onion, bellpepper, celery) sauteed with a lite cannabutter roux in a rich seafood broth and piled high with lump crabmeat.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup cannabutter
  • 1 white onion(finely chopped)
  • 1/4 red bellpepper
  • 6 celery ribs(finely chopped)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 5 cups seafood broth
  • 2 tablespoons of roasted garlic
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt, pepper, and thyme to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • parsley(garnish)
  • 2 pounds lump crab meat
Steps
  1. In a large pot, melt cannabutter. Add (Holy Trinity) and bay leaves and cook until soft. Stir in flour and cook to a creamy coffee brown color. Gradually whisk in seafood broth and bring to a boil. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until the broth is thickend.
  2. Add the roasted garlic and milk; bring to a simmer. Stir in the tomato paste and cream. Season with salt and pepper. Discard bay leaves. Add 1 lb crab and puree. Top with remaining lump crabmeat and Bon Appetit!
