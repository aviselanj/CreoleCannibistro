Creole Cannabistro

Gonja Gumbo

Cannabis infused file gumbo

Good gumbo starts with a good roux and we start our roux with equal parts Cannabutter and flour.

Our Cannabutter roux is suateed with the Creole Holy Trinity of onion, bellpepper, and celery to chocolatety perfection and married with a rich seafood broth. Blue crab, oysters, and shrimp swim in a melody of smoked sausage, beef, and ham.

As my Nana would say,"Na dat gumbo talking!"

Ingrediant List
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Cannabutter
  • 1 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 large finely chopped onion
  • 1 large finely chopped bell pepper
  • 3, 4, or 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 pound smoked sausage
  • 3 quarts seafood broth
  • salt, pepper to taste
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 3 tablespoons of file powder
  • 10 oz chopped okra
  • 1 pound lump crabmeat
  • 3 pounds shrimp
  • 2 pounds blue crab
  • 1 pound oysters
  • 1 pound of beef stew meat
  • half a pound of chopped ham
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons bacon drippings
Steps
  1. Make a roux by whisking the flour and Cannabutter together in a large, heavy saucepan over medium-low heat to form a smooth mixture. Cook the roux mixing constantly until it turns a rich chocolatety brown. Remove from heat; continue whisking until mixture stops cooking.
  2. Stir the finely chopped vegetables into the roux and mix in the stew meat, chopped ham, and smoked sausage. Bring the mixure to simmer over medium-low heat and cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Add seafood broth to roux mixture. Reduce heat to simmer. Add salt, pepper, and bay leaves. Simmer for 2 hours.
  4. Meanwhile, melt bacon drippings in skillet and sautee okra until no longer slimmy!!!(most important step). Remove okra with slotted spoon and transfer into simmering gumbo. Mix in blue crab, shrimp, oysters, and lump crabmeat and simmer about 30 more minutes. Stir in file powder and serve. Now pass dat Gonja!
